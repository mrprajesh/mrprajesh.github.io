Research

I am really fortunate to have my Ph.D. advisors as N.S. Narayanaswamy (also my M.Tech. project's advisor) and Rupesh Nasre. Both of them are wonderful professors/researchers I have ever met. From them, I have not just learnt doing research but also about good human values and other life+soft skills.

Our area of research is Graph Algorithms. More specifically, accelerating the performance of (Parallel) Graph Algorithms on GPUs.

Quite recently we are working on these problems:

  • Accelerating algorithms (exact and approximate for NP problems) using GPU. // Current
  • Designing exact algorithm for NP-hard problems(Treewidth, Minimum fill-in, Steiner tree, Vertex Cover). // PACE
  • Solving Problems(such as Dominating set) on Grid. // Visulisation Tool for Algorithmic solving
  • Designing Dynamic Data Structures for Graph Problems // M.Tech
